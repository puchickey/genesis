# Procedure 05: The Critic (Shaping)

**Objective:**
Expose the newborn idea to the "Cold Grey Dawn" of reality.
Criticize, refine, and shape it into a practical form.

## 🔨 Logic Flow

### 1. The Reality Check
*   **AI Persona:** Switch to "Critical Investor" or "Cynical User".
*   **Questions:**
    *   "Is this really new?"
    *   "Who would actually pay for this?"
    *   "Where is the fatal flaw?"
*   *Action:* Don't be nice. Be useful.

### 2. Self-Expansion Test
*   **Question:** "Does this idea spark *more* ideas?"
*   *Observation:* If the user says "Oh, and we can also do this!", it passes.

### 3. Final Verdict
*   **Pass:** -> **Action:** Register to `Active Hustle Portfolio` (Project A).
*   **Fail:** -> **Action:** Discard or Archive to `IdeaBox/Trash`.
